Server Rules

Read these before jumping in so the server stays fair, active, and fun for everyone.

Last updated: 2026/6/16

In-Game Rules

  1. Keep chat clean of spam, flooding, and toxicity.
  2. Do not use any disallowed modifications.
    • This includes macros and autoclickers.
    • The Disallowed Mods list is a starting point for determining disallowed modifications.
    • Double-binding is considered a disallowed modification.
    • Using external programs (e.g. FilterKeys) to modify keyboard repeat delay / repeat rate is prohibited.
    • Any method to make mouse clicks bind to keyboard inputs is disallowed.
  3. Do not advertise.
    • You are NOT allowed to promote your stream and immediately stream something other than NightFall SMP.
  4. Do not threaten the safety of other players.
    • Doxing, threatening, or harassing any individuals is completely unacceptable.
  5. Do not intentionally exploit or lower server performance.
    • Please report any exploits in a Senior Inquiry ticket.
  6. Do not impersonate staff or mislead players.
  7. Do not use an inappropriate username or skin.
  8. No X-Ray.
